Branding & Website Re-Design

Bodhi Tree Yoga Studio

Bodhi Tree is one of Calgary's longest running yoga studies, located in the heart of downtown for 16 years. They are distinctive for their high customer loyalty and the spa-like atmosphere of their physical studio space.

Problem + Scope

Their website presented a disconnect from their in-person brand experience and I sought to bridge the gap and provide a holistic brand image.

in some kind of order: - re-branding the bodhi tree as a no-nonsense serene atmosphere
- their photography
- information architecture
- catering to their target demographic , playing to their strengths

the scope

- the website is what i will work on
- and i will use the consumer research that was already done as i have limited resources and time. Ideally, i'd like to go more in depth and do a deep dive into the industry as specific to Calgary and to their specific consumer demographics.
